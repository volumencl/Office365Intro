Introducing Microsoft Office 365

As part of the College Microsoft subscription, staff and students are able to make use of the Microsoft Office 365 online tools. This includes Word, Excel, Powerpoint and OneDrive. Staff and students are also entitled to download and install the latest Microsoft Office version free of charge, for as long as you are studying or working at the New College Lanarkshire.

To access your OneDrive, Word, Excel and much more login to Microsoft Office 365 at: https://portal.office.com/

Microsoft OneDrive

Microsoft OneDrive is part of Office 365 which is available to staff and students. Everyone gets 1TB of storage space during their time at the college and this can be accessed on every device with an internet connection. All students should save their work to the OneDrive as the primary data source but should also take regular backups using a device like a USB drive. As good practice USB drives should be avoided as your primary and only source of saving your work. Having all your College work on a USB drive which is corrupted, is unfortunately an all too regular occurrence.

Microsoft Teams

Microsoft Teams is part of Office 365 which is available to staff and students. You can create a team within Microsoft Teams which this will allow you to share documents with people internally and people externally from the college. Using Microsoft Teams will allow you the freedom to manage who has access to your team and documents without contacting ICT. Teams is a great unified communication and collaboration digital platform which can be accessed on any drive with an internet connection.
