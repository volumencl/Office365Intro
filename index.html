Staff Accounts & Passwords

Staff

Your username is Firstname.Lastname (eg John.Smith) and your initial password is your date of birth as 8 numbers (e.g. 25121970). You’ll change this password when you first log on and every 60 days afterwards, choosing a password different from your last 10 and one with 8 or more characters. If you need your password reset please contact the ICT Helpdesk. 

Your email address is: Firstname.Lastname@nclan.ac.uk eg John.Smith@nclan.ac.uk 

Please make sure you have read and fully understood the ICT Policy. A copy of the ICT Policy can be found be clicking here

When on campus and logged in, many of the ICT systems do not prompt you further for authentication – this is called single-sign-on some online service like Adobe will require you to input your email address to use the product. Outside campus, you may be prompted to sign in when accessing resources: follow the instructions accordingly.

Students

Your username is your student number (as printed on your student card) and your initial password is your date of birth as 8 numbers (e.g. 25121970). You’ll change this password when you first log on and every 60 days afterwards, choosing a password different from your last 10 and one with 8 or more characters. If you need your password reset lecturer, library staff and ICT Helpdesk can do this for you.

Your email address is: StudentNumber@my.nclan.ac.uk eg 30012345@my.nclan.ac.uk

When on campus and logged in, many of the ICT systems do not prompt you further for authentication – this is called single-sign-on some online service like Adobe will require you to input your email address to use the product. Outside campus, you may be prompted to sign in when accessing resources: follow the instructions accordingly.

Protecting Yourself & Security

If your account has 4 consecutive unsuccessful login attempts, your account will be locked for a 15 minute period. If you require your password to be reset this can be done by your lecture (during class time), library staff and ICT Helpdesk (ICTHelpdesk@nclan.ac.uk) can do this for you with a valid student card. Staff must contact the ICT Helpdesk. When you update your password please remember to update this on your mobile device if you have it setup for MyDay or Emails. 

You can change your password at anytime by logging onto any College PC, when logged in successfully:
1. press Ctrl + Alt + Del to enter the security screen
2. Click "Change a password"
3. Enter the old password. Then, enter a new, secure password and confirm it.

The College has methods in place to help secure you and your data however this is everyones responsibillity to play their role in staying safe and secure online. Your password must never be shared.
